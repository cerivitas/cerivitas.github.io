About Cozy Blossom

Cozy Blossom was founded in 2023 with one simple goal: to serve high-quality matcha in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

We believe that every cup of matcha is a story, and we are committed to making yours special.

Why Choose Us?

  • Hand-picked matcha from local Japanese farmers.
  • Experienced and friendly baristas.
    • Free Wi-Fi and a comfortable space to work or relax.
  • A wide selection of cakes and snacks.
